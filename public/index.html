How does the AI agent analyze my Amazon reports?

What languages does the AI support?

Your AI-Generated Solutions

Step-by-step instructions to resolve your Amazon issues

Inventory Management Issue

Problem: Low stock levels for your top 3 selling products

  • Immediately increase order quantity for ASINs B08XYZ1234, B08ABC5678, and B08DEF9012
  • Set up inventory alerts at 20% of current stock levels
  • Consider using Amazon's FBA Small and Light program for faster restocking
  • Forecast demand using SellerRescueHub's prediction tool

High Return Rate

Problem: 18% return rate on new product line (ASIN: B08NEWPROD)

  • Review product listing for accuracy in sizing information
  • Add 3 additional images showing product in use with size reference
  • Update product description to include exact measurements
  • Consider offering a sizing chart in the product images
  • Respond to negative reviews mentioning sizing issues

Advertising Optimization

Problem: Low ROAS (1.8) on Sponsored Product campaigns

  • Pause underperforming keywords with ACOS > 40%
  • Increase bids on top-performing keywords by 20%
  • Create new ad group targeting complementary products
  • Adjust daily budget to allocate more to high-performing campaigns
  • Enable dynamic bidding - up and down
